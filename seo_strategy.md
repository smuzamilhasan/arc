# SEO Strategy

## In scope
- Public marketing landing page (`/`)
- Brand-level crawlability assets that affect the public site (`robots.txt`, `sitemap.xml`, `llms.txt`, favicon, social preview metadata)

## Out of scope
- Authenticated product routes (`/onboard`, `/dashboard`, `/blueprint/**`, `/audit`, `/narrative`, `/platforms`, `/content`, `/ideas`, `/account`, `/admin`)
- Auth flows as ranking targets (`/sign-in`, `/sign-up`) though they are still relevant if the current setup makes them indexable unintentionally

## Target audience
- Individual professionals, founders, or executives improving how they appear in search and AI systems
